How we estimate your earning potentail

The default earnings estimates we display are for entire home listings with a guest capacity of 4. Estimates are based on booking data from the past 12 months for your area and are calculated by multiplying a nightly price by the total nights of occupancy ("Occupancy Rate"). The nightly prices used are based on the median of the average prices for the past 12 months (before expenses, fees, and taxes). Weekly estimates use 7 nights of occupancy.


The Occupancy Rates used to calculate estimates is based on the top 50% of listings as measured by how often they're booked in that area and this portion of listings is used to illustrate the earning potential for engaged hosts. Only listings with sufficient availability are considered. Where you provide information about your potential listing including latitude/longitude, number of bedrooms and room type, the earnings estimate we provide reflects that information. Amounts are estimates and not guaranteed. How much you actually earn will depend on your availability, price, listing characteristics and amenities, acceptance and cancellation rates, guest reviews, applicable legal restrictions, demand in your area, and other factors.
